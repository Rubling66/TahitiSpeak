## Cultural Contributions

TahitiSpeak is deeply rooted in Tahitian culture and language. We especially welcome contributions from:

- Native Tahitian speakers
- Cultural experts and historians
- Language educators and linguists
- Community members with traditional knowledge

### Cultural Content Guidelines

When contributing cultural content:

1. **Accuracy First**: Verify all cultural information with reliable sources
2. **Respect Traditions**: Honor the sacred nature of traditional knowledge
3. **Seek Guidance**: Consult with cultural experts when uncertain
4. **Proper Attribution**: Credit traditional knowledge sources appropriately
5. **Community Review**: Allow time for community feedback on cultural content

2. **Set Up Development**: Follow the [Development Setup Guide](../development/setup-guide.md)
